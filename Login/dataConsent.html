DATA PRIVACY CONSENT STATEMENT

Taking into account the BARANGAY’s principle of Nobility, Bravery and Integrity, the public and other government agencies and branches can be assured that the personal data and other sensitive data they entrusted to the Barangay shall be used with due diligence and prudence, for the sole purpose of issuing individual Community Tax Certificate, Business Permit, Barangay Clearance, and Certificate of Residency, which may include investigation of derogatory record pursuant to our mandate under RA 10867.

As such, upon accessing the BARANGAY’s online registration site and availing of our services you acknowledge and agree that the information may be used and disclosed by the Barangay in accordance with any legal and regulatory standards and in compliance with the “Data Privacy Act of 2012”
